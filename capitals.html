European Capitals



Barcelona

Capital of Spain. Known for being one of the most beautiful europe cities that are next to the beach.

Attractions:

  • Camp Nou
  • Sagrada Família


Budapest

Capital of Hungary. Known for surviving 800 years of earthquakes, fires and war without losing its great atmosphere.

Attractions:

  • Chain Bridge
  • Castle District


Lisbon

Capital of Portugal. The Cathedral of Lisbon and Triumph Arch are the most popular places that tourists visit.

Attractions:

  • Cathedral of Lisbon
  • Triumph Arch


Paris

Capital of France which is also known as the "City of Love". But there are dark secrets beneath the city streets...

Attractions:

  • Eiffel Tower
  • Arc de Triomphe
  • Louvre
  • Catacombs of Paris


Prague

Capital of the Czech Republic. Being built upon century old architecture from romanian times, this is one of the most popular capitals that Europe has to offer.

Attractions:

  • King's Palace
  • Charles Bridge
  • Vitus Cathedral
